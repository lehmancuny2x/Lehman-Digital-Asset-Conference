3nd Annual Lehman College

Digital Asset Symposium

October 29th, 2025 | 12:00 pm – 4:00 pm

Lehman College, Music Building, East Dining Room

REGISTER
DAYS HR MIN SEC

With technologies including but not limited to artificial intelligence, cryptoassets, and blockchain driving innovation and change across the business landscape, it is imperative that balanced and objective conversations continue to be held. At the Digital Asset Symposium there will be keynotes from industry leaders and four (4) panels discussing the various implications of these emerging technologies across business, academia, and the student journey. Held on the bucolic campus of Lehman College this event is free to all attendees and one not to miss!

Interested in participating?

In addition to submitting to be part of our panel conversations, we also invite you to submit appropriate article drafts or abstracts for inclusion in the conference proceedings. These submissions must be original work, and not be under consideration for publication elsewhere at the time or submission. For any questions please contact conference co-organizer Sean Stein Smith at sean.steinsmith@lehman.cuny.edu
