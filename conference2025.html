CONFERENCE AGENDA

October 29th, 2025 | 12:00 pm - 4:00 pm

12:15 PM – 1:00 PM

Panel 1 – Tokenization 2.0: Real-World Assets on the Blockchain

Explore tokenized U.S. treasuries, real estate, and private credit. Discuss regulatory shifts, stablecoins, and financial infrastructure.

● Institutional adoption of tokenized assets

● Stablecoins & reserve transparency

● Post-FIT21 and GENIUS Act implications

1:05 PM – 1:50 PM

General Session – AI Across Industries: Disruption, Deployment, and ROI

Learn how AI is being implemented in finance, logistics, healthcare, and more—along with ROI and risk management challenges.

● AI use cases across sectors

● Measuring return on AI investments

● Managing risks: bias, security, workforce shifts

1:55 PM – 2:40 PM

GENERAL SESSION: Educating for an AI Future: Teaching the Next Gen of Innovatorse

How should schools and colleges adapt to an AI-powered world? This session explores how educators are building digital fluency, embedding ethics, and preparing learners for a tech-driven economy.

● AI in classrooms and higher education

● Curriculum development for digital literacy

● Critical thinking vs. AI hype in education

2:45 PM – 3:30 PM

PANEL 2: AI + Crypto Convergence: What the Future Holds

As autonomous AI agents begin engaging with blockchain ecosystems, a new frontier of decentralized intelligence is emerging. This panel explores the practical and policy implications of this convergence.

● AI agents in crypto transactions

● DeFi enhanced by AI and predictive models

● Regulatory responses to intelligent automation

3:35 PM – 4:00 PM

NETWORKING RECEPTION

Join us for light refreshments and meaningful conversations with panelists, tech leaders, students, and faculty.
